Use the blue boxes below to do a query search of the data available (done) and in the download queue (queued) for CMIP6.

Note the change in attribute labels from CMIP5 to CMIP6. For example, 'model' in CMIP5 is now referred as 'source_id' in CMIP6 and 'ensemble' in CMIP5 is 'member_id'.

Both the download status and priorities are shown. A priority of 1-2 indicates variables in the bulk download while a priority of 'U' indicates that it has been requested by a user.

If the data you require is not found, please see the 'https://opus.nci.org.au/pages/viewpage.action?spaceKey=CMIP&title=Data+Download+Request' for details on making a download request.
